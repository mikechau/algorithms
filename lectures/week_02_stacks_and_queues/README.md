## Definitions

- `Stack` - Examine the item most recently added, also known as **LIFO**, *last in first out*.
    + Push
    + Pop

- `Queue` - Examine the item least recently added, also known as **FIFO**, *first in first out*.
    + Queue
    + Dequeue
